WELCOME TO MARCELLOS

Marcellos Restaurant serves a wide range of authentic Italian dishes with a contemporary flair, with many awards and nominations over the last 20 years, showcases the gastronomic heritage of Italy with critically acclaimed pizza, pasta and meat or fish dishes. We offer a relaxed dining experience at our restaurant, situated in the heart of Sunderland. Expect at Marcellos Restaurant delicious food, excellent wine, and a relaxing atmosphere. Marcello’s team are always on hand to give a taste of little Italy to make your dining experience one that you will be sharing with your friends, keeping you returning time and time again. From romantic meals to family outings, working lunches or big parties, Marcellos offers something for everyone!
